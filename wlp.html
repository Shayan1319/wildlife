WildLife Refuge Parks!

Wildlife Refuge is an area set aside to provide safe heaven to the species of wildlife. Hunting & shooting of all wildlife species found in a refuge is strictly prohibited. WildLife Refuge Parks!

Wildlife Park is usually a fenced area set aside for the rehabilitation of endangered wildlife species under semi natural condition. The wildlife park is also used for conservation education and recreation of the public. The following acts are prohibited in a wildlife park.

  • Hunting, killing or capturing of a wild species or firing any gun or any other fire arms.
  • Polluting the water.
  • Damaging or destruction of vegetation
